Enjoy relaxing in our traditional bar or dine in our restaurant with an open fire. Our delicious specials are changed every Friday so that you have extra choice and variety for your meal.

Join us for our weekly events at the Buccaneer including our Wednesday night quiz, which includes Free supper. Friday evenings prove that we are the 'Best in Brough' as the bar fills up every week from teatime onwards as everyone is ready for a relaxing drink after a long hard week.
